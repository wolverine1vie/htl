Who We Are

TrioTone blends rhythm, soul, and electronic edge in a unique sonic fusion.

Markus — Vocal & Guitar

Born in Vienna into a Family of Musicians, rythm and vibes have been part of his life since he was a child. After beeing part of various previous bands and experimenting with his sound he finally build The Danube Three with close friends he has known for year. Know for his hoarse baritone voice and dramatic deliveries he is ready to deliver captivating and soulseizing records

Ali — Percussion

Ali who is know for his almost virtuoso-like mastery of his drums and a fan favourite therefor is the backanchor of this band. His craft is a mix of natural talent and endless passionate rehearsings. Musik is a way of venting my innermost feelings and sharing them with the world is one of his notable quotes

Tanja — Vocal, Bassline & Synthscape

With her melo, soothing yet energetic voice she is memorable performances. Born to iranian Parents and political Refugees she came to Austria when she was just 5 years old. She often reflects her cultural diversity in her songs. Her extovert nature and fiery delivery she always light up the stage
